Does the app work offline?
Yes. The last fetched rate is cached on your device so the app works even without internet. You'll see a yellow indicator if the cached rate is more than 12 hours old.

What does "new SYP" mean?
It's the displayed amount divided by 100, useful for the common practice of dropping two zeros when discussing prices.
